Milly's Eyelash Extensions

April 05, 2017

Having long, luscious eyelashes is like every girl's #goals today lol - Including mine. Sadly, as a very typical Asian I'm not blessed with them naturally and that's why I'm extremely dependent on eyelash extensions.

If you're looking for something natural, I would recommend the Royal Furlash Extensions (Single Strand Lashes, retails at $98-$108 @ Milly's) in order to achieve thicker and fuller lashes. It feels light on your eyes and falls naturally overtime.

It's widely known as the "single lash extensions" where lashes are placed strand by strand on your natural lashes. This is the one I would usually go for as it's extremely comfortable and helps open up your eyes.

I guess what I love most about this is that it shortens your makeup routine (mascara can be a pain in the ass when it comes to both the application and removal process) + it does not damage your real lashes!

Great for first timers who are looking for something natural and comfy!
For those who are looking for extremely thick/falsies-like lashes, I would definitely recommend going for Cluster Lashes instead. The process takes a lot faster and cost slightly cheaper. $70-$92 @ Milly's.

By the way, if you're afraid of your lashes falling off, the best part would be free touch ups! Milly's provide a 7-day complimentary touch-up for all cluster lashes and unlimited single strand lashes!
